About Yakshagana and Drama Club

Participating in drama club activities can help students develop their communication skills, boost their confidence, and increase their cultural awareness. It can also provide an opportunity for them to explore their creativity and express themselves through different art forms. Overall, drama clubs play an important role in preserving the rich heritage of Yakshagana and other traditional arts in India.
